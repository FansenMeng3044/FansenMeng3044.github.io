## Projects

### ORD: Object-Relation Decoupling for Generalized 3D Visual Grounding
Working on anchor-centric relative geometry modeling and predicate-level relation disentanglement.

### Calibration for VLMs
Studying calibration-set selection for post-training compression of vision-language models and exploring data generation for better compression-performance trade-offs.

### HairFlow: Hair Reconstruction with Continuous Orientation Fields
Built a continuous-orientation-field hair reconstruction system with flow-consistent latent refinement and hierarchical geometric constraints.

### HRF: Hyper-Relation Fusion for MDVRP/MDOVRP
Developed a dual-GNN framework that fuses homogeneous and heterogeneous relations into hyper-relation embeddings.

### RL-based Neural Combinatorial Optimization for Multi-Robot Task Allocation
Designed a deep reinforcement learning policy network with heterogeneous-attention encoder and robot-selection decoding.
